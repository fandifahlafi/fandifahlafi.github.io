THE BEGINNING OF THE JOURNEY

22 April 2018

Starting with the screening and discussion of the film at the Situs Pojok Persada Soekarno, film activists followed up by forming the Whatsapp Sinema Kadirian group as a communication forum for film activists.

They are film activists students and alumni. After a number of discussions, the Sinema Kadirian activists finally agreed to organize activities: screening and discussion of the film Marlina the Murderer in Four Acts.
From just a forum, finally followed up with the formation of the organization. Attended by Danu Sukendro, Rahman and Muhammad Prasetyo as founder, Muhammad Adde Rheno was chosen as the chairman of the organization

Going forward, Sinema Kadirian has a great enthusiasm for greater breakthroughs. For the sake of stretching the film in Kediri.

At our first event, the 2018 FFI award-winning film and the 2018 Asian World Film Festival; Marlina the Murderer in Four Acts will be the subject of our appreciation and discussion. The discussion was held on December 29, 2018, at BBSTV Kediri Station with speakers who were experts in their fields. Among them are Sony Sudarsono Videography Practitioner, Iwan Kapit Literacy Activist, and Premy Bimbim Film Maker Hisstory Film.
